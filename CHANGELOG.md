## 1.0 (build 9) — 2026-06-13

- First-run welcome note: turn off the Apple TV system screen saver for an
  uninterrupted view (also stated in Settings)
- The Wine-Dark exclusive is now a one-time ceremonial release — a hero work
  surfacing from a wine-dark deep with a line of Homer and the sea's music —
  rather than another gallery room; opening it spends it, and it leaves the
  lobby when you return
- About: rewritten around the Institute's philosophy and vision (a school, not
  a tools shop)

## 1.0 (build 8) — 2026-06-13

- Top shelf is now a salon-hung gallery wall of the real masterpieces, each in
  an era-appropriate frame (9 works wide / 6 narrow)
- Era-appropriate frame styles: added `baroque` (carved Salon gold), `dutch`
  (ebonized black with a gold sight-line), and `secession` (flat Klimt gold);
  reassigned each work to what it would actually hang in
- Rooms now hang the work near full-screen with a richer side placard, a
  position indicator, and visible left/right navigation
- Slow Ken Burns drift on each hung work (honors Reduce Motion)
- The colophon credits Wine-Dark and the San Diego Institute of Technology — and
  hides the door to "The Wine-Dark Sea," an exclusive wing with its own oxblood
  wall, found by pressing the wine-dark seal five times
- Settings: select "San Diego Institute of Technology" for a quick About overview
